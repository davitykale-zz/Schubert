The Mead Presents: The Schubert Quintet Sound Installation

Come learn about the Schubert project at the Mead and engage with the sound installation at the Rotherwas room. Five speakers, positioned in a large oval configuration, will encourage visitors to walk among hte speakers and heaer individual instruments, interplay among instruments, or the whole. Whereas musicans often experience this sensation as a part of an ensemble, this installation offers a similar opportunity to hear one instrument at a time, or in groups of two or three. This experience will take place not just in any room, but in this special wood panelled room finished in 1611 and reassembled at Amherst in the 20th century. The installation opens Tuesday, August 26th and runs until Monday, September 22nd.
